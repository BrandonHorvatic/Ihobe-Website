Dust Suppression Solutions

Our concept is revolutionizing the dust suppression industry using science and innovative technology to improve decision making and effectiveness of product in increasingly unpredictable climatic patterns. Through the current issues that are prevalent in the mining industry, in the context of dust suppression and road stabilization, Ihobe has undertaken an alternative approach in comparison to traditional dust palliatives.

We have identified the core issues from mining corporations and dust suppression companies and integrated these concerns to create a simplistic, cost effective product, called Profit Science Liquid. Dust suppression companies have been viewed as:

  • Requirements of specialized equipment and staff
  • High operational costs
  • Increased health and safety risks

To eradicate these issues, a Family of Profit Science Liquid (PSL) was developed around a concept of simplicity and cost efficiency. Our latest technology is a member of our polymer family that has been designed with ultra-hydrophobicity. The suitability and effectiveness of the product allows mine corporations to improve decision making and overall productivity of their operation.
